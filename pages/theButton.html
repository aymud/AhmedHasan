Project Name: The Delightful Button

Visual Summary

Project Description

A desktop game application created with a single button with the hopes of evoking wonder and delight to my client who loves cats and adding fancy RBG lights to her PC build.

My Client

The topics I focused my interview questions around were: "What brings you delight during COVID" and "Something in your house that you enjoy interacting with".



Design Rationale

Inspired from the idea of some fancy lighting and cats that my client liked. Her cat was a rescue cat leading me to create a game where you save falling cats. Also, she liked managing cables when building her PC so, I decided to use snakes as an enemy to represent tangled cables.





Interaction Design

Playing around with the different events that happen when you interact with a button led me to design it in such a way so that the user feels engaged and that they have full control over the button.
